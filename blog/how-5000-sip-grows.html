Key Insights

  • Starting early reduces financial pressure later.
  • Long-term consistency beats short-term timing.

If average return is 10% instead of 12%, the final corpus will be lower, but long-term investing still creates significant wealth.

Final Conclusion

A ₹5,000 monthly SIP may look small today, but over 25–30 years it can create substantial wealth. The key factor is consistency and patience.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is 12% return guaranteed?

No. Mutual fund returns depend on market performance.

2. Can I increase SIP amount later?

Yes. Step-up SIP increases investment annually and accelerates growth.

3. Is ₹5,000 enough to start?

Yes. Starting small is better than delaying investment.

4. What happens if I miss a SIP?

Missing occasional installments may reduce total returns but does not cancel the investment.
